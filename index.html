About

Four years ago, Tom Howard and the team at Lee's Air saw a great need in the Central Valley and decided to fill it. What they saw was homeowners looking for exceptional, local businesses to service and maintian their homes. There were plenty of companies offering a maintenance program, but none of them were hitting that exceptional customer service mark that Fresno needed.

To answer the call for greatness, Lee's Air started the "Club Advantage" program. This program gave customers security and peace of mind that their air conditioning, heating, and plumbing needs would always be taken care of. Come freezing winter or scorching summer, the team at Lee's Air & Plumbing had their back!

Now, as 2016 presses on, Lee's has seen an ever-growing demand for our exceptional customer service. Customers have even began to expect it from other companies they work with, often asking the Lee's team who they might recommend. Fresno Advantage is the answer

Fresno Advantage is a joining-of-fources, if you will. They very best companies in the valley offering the best in customer service have teamed up to bring you one Advantage. This program includes everything the traditional "Lee's Club Advantage intales" and adds more percs and discounts from our partners. Read below for more information!
